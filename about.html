ABOUT COMPANY

Researched, Developed and manufactured 100% entirely in India, by highly qualified Metallurgists from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Bombay - Spangle Alloys meets the International Quality Standards. As a testimony to this fact, our Alloys have been found compatible by most of the Export oriented jewelry manufacturing companies in India and other major Jewelry hubs in Asia Pacific.

MISSION

To deliver to the international market superior, long-term value to our customers, mainly manufacturers of gold and silver jewelry, providing them innovative products and services, aimed at satisfying their production requirements domestically as well as very much globally.

To be the customer's first choice for master alloys in the jewelry industry, offering the best material and technology available on the global market.

To provide know-how and consultation for any customer's production activity, acting as an education and training centre for its technical staff.

Spangle strives to set the benchmark for the industry in the following key areas:

Total Customer Satisfaction

Proven Superior Quality

Technological Leadership

PROMOTER

The key promoter of Spangle Alloys Pvt. Ltd., (SAPL), Rajesh Dagli, (RD), a metallurgist from I.I.T. Bombay, started his career in 1974 as a co-founder and CEO of Jewel Metallochem Laboratory (JML) - a metallurgical test house of a very high repute. Under his leadership, JML's worth grew to nearly a thousand fold increase in 5 years period; not only that, JML carved its place among the top three laboratories in the Western India. However, the spirit of entrepreneurship didn't stop here. Two more ventures were started by RD; one was of a hallmarking laboratory for gold jewellery, and the other of manufacturing alloys for gold and silver jewellery. Needless to say, in a very short time, both the ventures proved a great success. The hall marking activities are being carried out in the name of Leo Analytics Lab; and the alloys manufacturing activities in the name of Spangle Alloys Pvt. Ltd.
